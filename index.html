About Us

A Cozy Spot,
Brewed with Heart

At Pink Bear Café, we believe every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of the city, our café is more than just a coffee shop — it's a warm haven where creativity meets comfort, and every visitor feels like family.

Whether you're craving our signature rose latte, a slice of our famous strawberry cake, or simply a cozy corner to unwind, Pink Bear is your perfect escape. We source ethically, brew passionately, and serve lovingly.
