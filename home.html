About Us

A Cozy Corner for Coffee, Comfort, and Community

Pink Bear began as a simple idea: create a café that feels warm, welcoming, and a little bit magical. We wanted a place where the coffee is comforting, the details are thoughtful, and every visit feels personal.

From creamy signature drinks to soft pink touches and relaxed seating, everything is designed to invite you to stay a little longer. Whether you're meeting friends, working quietly, or taking a break from the day, Pink Bear is your cozy neighborhood escape.

More than a café, we’re a community spot — a place for conversation, creativity, and everyday rituals shared over a really good cup of coffee.
